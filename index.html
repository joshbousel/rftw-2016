Register to run or walk on your own, with a friend, or as part of a team. Choose from one of two start-times to take off with either the “Runners” or as part of the “Family Fun Run/Walk.” (If you plan to come with your family or a group, you must register as a team.) You’ll also receive free general admission to the Bronx Zoo for the day with your registration. Or if you can't be at the Bronx Zoo on April 25th, become a virtual runner.

Then, set up a personal page and start fundraising! The suggested fundraising goal is $275 and the more you raise the more prizes you’ll be eligible to receive. You can even kick-start your fundraising by making a donation to help gorillas with your registration register – and remember, all adult (and senior) participants are required to raise at least $30 in order to participate!
