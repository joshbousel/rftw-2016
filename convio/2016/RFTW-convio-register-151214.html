Make tracks for gorillas in the 2015 WCS Run for the Wild at the Bronx Zoo. This year's 5K run/walk takes place on Saturday, April 25. We’ll have two start times, one for runners at 8:00 a.m., and a second for our Family Fun Run/Walk at 8:45 a.m.

Registration

Before January 30th
$40 for adults
$30 for children (3–15)
$30 for Seniors (65+)

January 31st - April 25th
$45 for adults
$35 for children (3–15)
$35 for Seniors (65+)

Fundraising Requirement

All adults and senior registrants must also raise at least $30 before the run in order to participate on April 25! The minimum fundraising requirement is waived for children. 

Perks & Prizes

All registered adults and seniors who meet the $30 fundraising requirement will receive:

  • Entrance to either the 5K or Family Fun Run
  • Complimentary Bronx Zoo general admission and parking for the entire day
  • A commemorative WCS Run for the Wild t-shirt

You’ll also be eligible for a host of prizes based on your personal fundraising success: You can win a gorilla plush, blanket, fleece, and a VIP breakfast. Sign up and get a jump start on your fundraising now.
