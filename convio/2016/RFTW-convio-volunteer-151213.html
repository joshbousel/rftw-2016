Want to make tracks for gorillas, but prefer not to run? Sign up as a volunteer and you’ll help the event run smoothly. Your services are greatly appreciated. Here are some areas that volunteers may be assigned:

  • Greeting participants
  • Helping direct traffic and parking
  • Handing out t-shirts and bibs
  • Bag check
  • Start line directors
  • Fulfillment center for fundraising prizes
  • Finish line directors
  • Serving food
  • Staffing the water stations
  • Serving as course directors

Perks include:

  • WCS Run for the Wild Volunteer t-shirt
  • Refreshments
  • General Admission to the Bronx Zoo

And, of course, you’ll be helping WCS save gorillas and other wildlife at the Bronx Zoo and around the globe. For more information or to join the WCS Run for the Wild Volunteer Team, please submit the form below.
