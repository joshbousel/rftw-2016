Thank you for signing up for the 6th annual Run for the Wild at the Bronx Zoo. Get ready for a fun-filled day of activity!

But before race day, you have some preparation to do. Here's a quick checklist of things you'll want to do to make sure you're ready and making tracks to save wildlife from now till April 29! 

Don't wait to start your fundraising – all adult participants must raise at least $30 by race day in order to participate.

In addition to participation in the run, you'll also get a fashionable WCS Run for the Wild t-shirt and admission to the Bronx Zoo for the day.

Raise even more and you could earn several fabulous prizes. The clock is ticking, so get started TODAY!

  1. Start by visiting your personal participant center and customizing your personal page. You'll find all kinds of fundraising tips and great tools there. Bookmark that page and visit often.
  2. Kick-start your fundraising effort by making a small gift to yourself Remember, all adult participants must raise $30 by race day – and when you hit $30,  you'll have earned a free, commemorative race shirt! And think of the difference you'll be making for wildlife. Even a $10 donation will show your friends and family that you're committed to securing a future for wildlife and wild places.
  3. Customize your fundraising goal – your friends and family are going to be motivated to help you hit it so be sure to set your sights high for wildlife! Head to your participant center and you'll see your goal of $0 displayed beneath the Fundraising Progress bar. Just click “change” and enter your new goal level!
  4. Add our simple Facebook App and put your profile to work to help you meet your fundraising goal. It will help you build momentum among your friends in the lead up to race day.

Good luck in your efforts and we can't wait to see you on April 29!

Sincerely,
