Rock Wall

  • Must be 7 years or older to climb wall
  • Must sign a waiver to climb the wall
  • If under 18 years old, must be accompanied by a parent or guardian
  • 250lb weight limit
  • All climbers must be harnessed
  • No sandals or bare feet
  • Anyone who has been drinking alcohol is not permitted to climb the wall
  • The line to climb the wall closes at 12:00pm
