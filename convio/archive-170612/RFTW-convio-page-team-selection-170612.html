Start a Team

Start a team to make tracks for wildlife - your team can consist of your family, friends, and/or co-workers. You'll be the Team Captain in charge of creating the team name, recruiting members, and establishing a fundraising goal. You'll also set up two fundraising pages, one for yourself and one for the team.

Begin by entering your team name and fundraising goal in the box at the bottom of this page. Then, complete your registration form. Once you have submitted your own registration, you will have the opportunity to register additional teammates.

Next, set up a team page to start fundraising! The suggested fundraising goal is $300 per team member or $1,500 per team, and the more you raise, the more prizes you'll be eligible to receive. You can even kick-start your fundraising by making a donation to help wildlife with your registration.

Note: If you would like to join an existing team rather than start your own, please click here.

*Remember, fundraising prizes are awarded to individuals, not teams.

Join a Team

To join an existing team consisting of your family, friends, and/or co-workers, enter its name (or the first few letters) in the "Team Name" field at the bottom of this page. Then click on the "Search for a Team" button at the bottom left. Scroll through the results that appear beneath it to select the team you would like to join.
