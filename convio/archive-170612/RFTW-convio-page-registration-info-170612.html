Run for wildlife and wild places at WCS’s 2016 Run for the Wild at the Bronx Zoo. This year’s 5K run/walk takes place on Saturday, April 29th. We’ll have two start times, one for runners at 8:00 a.m., and a second for our Family Fun Run/Walk at 8:45 a.m.

Registration for WCS Run for the Wild at the Bronx Zoo is $45 for adults, $35 for children (3-16) and $35 for Seniors (65+).

All adults and senior registrants must also raise at least $30 before the run in order to participate on April 30! The fundraising requirement is waived for children.

And don’t wait – the Run regularly sells out early. Secure your spot and register today!

Can't be at the Bronx Zoo on April 29th? You can still run to support wildlife and wild places by becoming a virtual runner. Just commit to running or walking on your own — around town, on your treadmill, even at another 5K. When you raise $30, you’ll receive the event t-shirt; raise more and you’ll qualify for a host of great fundraising prizes!

For questions about parking, strollers, weather conditions, matching gifts, and more, read our FAQs.
