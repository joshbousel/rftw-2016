Make tracks for tigers in 2010 with the Wildlife Conservation Society’s Run for the Wild on Saturday, April 24, 2010 at the Bronx Zoo.

Join us for the second annual Run for the Wild.  Families, runners, corporations, people of all ages, are invited to run, hop, skip, crawl or slither to the Bronx Zoo for what proves to be one the most unique runs in New York.

The 5K course takes participants on a journey throughout the beautiful Bronx Zoo grounds and home to over 5,000 animals from around the world.  This year, the Run for the Wild is dedicated to tigers.  Fundraising efforts will support WCS’s long standing conservation goals to create protected areas where tigers can thrive by working with local governments and reducing threats such as illegal hunting, hunting of tigers’ prey species, and destruction of their habitats.

Where else in New York can you get fit, have fun, raise money and awareness for an endangered species and then enjoy an up close encounter with tigers? 

This year we are featuring two dedicated start times:

Runners at 8:30
Family Fun Run/Walk at 9:00am

Can’t participate in the event?  You can still take action for wildlife by making a donation!

Individuals

Individuals are welcome to join the Run for the Wild by running or walking on their own or with friends! Online registration is $30, and the suggested fundraising goal is $250. You’ll receive a 2010 Run for the Wild T-shirt and complimentary Bronx Zoo general admission.  Choose from the “Runners” or “Family Fun Run” start times.  You’ll be part of a fun event while helping to save tigers too!

Start a team

Start a team and recruit as many members as you wish and make tracks for tigers!  You’re a  “conservation team captain” in charge of creating a team name, recruiting members, establishing a fundraising goal and setting up your team page and personal page.

Online registration is $30 for adults
The suggested fundraising goal is $250 per team member

There is no participation limit - recruit as many teammates as you like – all ages welcome! As part of your registration fees, you and all your registered team members will receive 2010 Run for the Wild 2010 run T-shirts and complimentary admission to the Bronx Zoo.  Be eligible for prizes for the top fundraising team!
