You've chosen an animal to run for and you’ve started training. Now the tricky part: fundraising. We know sometimes your people might need a little extra push to chip in. That’s why we’re here.

Between now and April 3, every $10 you raise from family, friends, or colleagues equals one chance for you and your donor to win an incredible vacation package.

You could win a 7-night luxury cruise for two in the South Pacific, courtesy of Paul Gauguin Cruises, plus roundtrip flights to Tahiti.

If you win, you'll have a chance to explore the best of the Society Islands, including Huahine, Bora Bora, and Moorea—each of which features unique wildlife, atmosphere, and soul.

That's right. You have a chance to win a trip of a lifetime just by fundraising for the WCS Run for the Wild. The more money you raise, the more chances you have to win, thanks to our sponsor.

So get out there and spread the word. And get ready to make tracks for animals on Saturday, April 30.
