By participating in the WCS Run for the Wild, you're already doing a lot to keep up your health. Here are some simple tips, recipes and information for wellness courtesy of Montefiore Medical Center.


Eat a Meal or Snack Before Exercising (click to download the pdf) >>

Tips:

  1. Don't eat anything new or unusual before an event. Ê
  2. Make sure to eat at least 12 hours before the event. A balanced breakfast includes easy-to-digest carbohydrates and protein.
  3. Make it a smoothie! A smoothie can be an energy-filled, nutritious and delicious breakfast option. Just be careful to not go overboard with the ingredients so you are not taking in more calories than you could possibly burn.
