Virtual Runner Registration

It's super easy to register to run or walk on your own, with a friend, or as part of a team on April 30. As a virtual runner, you can make the 5k Run for the Wild anywhere!

Just select one of the options below to get started - and be sure to select the "Virtual Runner" option during your registration process. This option will let you sign up with no fee.

Registering should only take you a few minutes and will give you access to all kinds of tools to make your run a success, as well as make you eligible for a host of great prizes.

  • Start a Team

    Sign up as a “team captain” in charge of recruiting members and establishing a fundraising goal. Then, set up your team and personal pages.

  • Start a New Team

  • Join a Team

    Search for the team you want to join using the team name. Then, register and set up a personal page to start your fundraising.

  • Join an Existing Team

  • Virtual Runners

    Help save gorillas by committing to run or walk on your own — around town, at the gym, even at another 5K. When you raise $30, you’ll receive our t-shirt; raise more to earn great fundraising prizes

  • Participate as a Virtual Runner
