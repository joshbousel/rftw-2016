All registered children and adults who meet the $30 fundraising requirement, will get:

  • Entrance to either the 5K or Family Fun Run
  • Complimentary Bronx Zoo general admission and parking for the entire day
  • A commemorative WCS Run for the Wild t-shirt
[[S51:reus_rftw_prize_list]]

All participants are also eligible for a host of prizes based on your personal fundraising success: You can win an animal plush, water bottle, tote bag, yoga mat, Bronx Zoo Total Experience tickets, a VIP brunch, and a "Wild Life" print bag courtesy of Sakroots. Sign up and get a jump start on your fundraising now.
